# Routines

## Morning

- Wake up without negotiating with the ceiling.
- Water, medication, coffee, brief stretch.
- Read Schedule and CurrentProjects before looking at messages.
- Write one paragraph in Daily if the day already feels slippery.

## Weekly

- One review of work, mood, health, and money.
- Reset inboxes, calendar, loose paper, and the "I will remember this" pile.
- Decide the one project that matters next week.

## Evening

- Shut down work on purpose instead of by collapse.
- Leave a short Daily note for tomorrow-me.
- Dim lights, read fiction, and stop auditioning for new worries after midnight.
