# Schedule

## Ideal Week

- Monday to Thursday: 4 focused work hours, 1 admin block, movement, simple meals.
- Friday: review, planning, errands, loose creative exploration.
- Saturday: social life, long walk, no fake urgency.
- Sunday: reset the apartment, write, and re-enter the week gently.

## Daily Rhythm

- Morning: orientation, planning, deepest work.
- Afternoon: collaboration, logistics, calls, and errands.
- Evening: recovery, relationships, reading, and the sort of reflection that does not require a spreadsheet.
