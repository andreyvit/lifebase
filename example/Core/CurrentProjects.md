# Current Projects

## Harbor Atlas

A fictional neighborhood map and local-memory app. The goal is to make a city feel legible, affectionate, and full of texture instead of like a spreadsheet with weather.

## The Velvet Alarm Essays

A fictional essay series about ambition, stress, self-deception, and the absurd pageantry of modern knowledge work.

## House of Useful Habits

A deliberately dramatic name for a very ordinary goal: rebuild sleep, meals, exercise, and weekly review so life stops feeling like a heroic emergency.
